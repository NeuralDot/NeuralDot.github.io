Space Junk

Overview

Come, let out the inventive genius in you! The task is to build a working model from scratch. Teams have to buy the required components from a huge pile of electronic components like RaspberryPi, Arduino, Sensors etc, using Virtual Money. Teams will have to plan out, make the right buying and selling decisions to get the best suited components and come up with the final model within the given time.

Details

  • Venue :
  • Team of : 3
  • Registration fees : ₹ 200 per team
  • Price Money : 1st - 2000 INR ; 2nd - 1000 INR

Round 1

Complete list of components available in the event will be displayed and in the given amount of time the participants can discuss and buy components by making use of Virtual money( Fixed amount will be given to all the participants) to come up with a working model. The participants will be given internet access. Mentor advice can be sought using Virtual Money.

Round 2

Presentation :Each team has to present and demonstrate their model (5 minutes/team).
