Space Pellicule

" Lights Camera Action! "

Overview

Imagination brings to life an awe-inspiring new ideas. This event is an open opportunity inspiring the art of video making. Grab your cameras and bring life to amazing ideas running inside your head by participating in this competition.Its a two day event.

Details

  • Venue :
  • Team of : 3-4
  • Registration fees : ₹ 200 per team
  • Price Money : 1st - 3000 INR ; 2nd - 1500 INR

ROUND 1 : Make Remake

Remaking of reference video given(The video will be shared to participants 3 days before the event). The participants should remake the reference video and present it on the first day of event.

Round 2 : Tech Buzz

A questionnaire round based on technical aspects of movie making. Hints will be given to each question .Elimination after 2nd round is based on the performances of both 1st and 2nd round. Ex: Video Exposure control

Round 3

The Review Problem statement will be given to qualified teams and a day time, a short video of 5mins max based on the advantages and disadvantages of the given issue and solutions to the same.
